How to make pasta?
  1. Fill a large saucepan with water, put the lid on and bring to the boil over a high heat
  2. Add a good pinch of sea salt
  3. Once the water is boiling
    1. Stir in the pasta
    2. Cook the pasta according to the packet instructions
    3. It's ready when it's soft enough to eat, but still has a bit of bite
  4. Enjoy!
